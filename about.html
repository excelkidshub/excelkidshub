About ExcelKidsHub Phonics Academy

Building confident young readers in Dhanori, Pune

Our Story

ExcelKidsHub Phonics Academy was founded with a clear mission — to help children become confident, independent readers at an early age.

After years of teaching experience, we observed that many children struggled with reading not because they lacked ability, but because they lacked a strong phonics foundation. This inspired the creation of a structured learning space focused purely on systematic phonics instruction.

Why ExcelKidsHub Was Started

  • ✔ Proper structured phonics foundation
  • ✔ Small batches for individual attention
  • ✔ Scientific and step-by-step reading methods
  • ✔ Confidence-building learning environment

Located in Dhanori, Pune, ExcelKidsHub was created to support parents who want strong literacy skills for their children from the beginning.

Our Teaching Philosophy

We believe reading is a skill that must be taught systematically. Every child learns at a different pace, and structured phonics ensures steady progress.

  • • Letter sound recognition
  • • Blending & segmenting
  • • Reading fluency
  • • Spelling improvement
  • • Reading comprehension

Meet the Founder

Swati Deshmukh is a UK-Certified Jolly Phonics Trainer with over 10 years of teaching experience in English and early childhood education.

Her goal is not just to teach phonics, but to build strong academic foundations that help children succeed confidently in school and beyond.
