Our Story

ExcelKidsHub Phonics Academy was established with a clear mission — to provide structured and scientific phonics education that builds confident and independent readers from an early age.

Many children struggle with reading not because they lack ability, but because they miss strong foundational instruction. We created ExcelKidsHub to bridge that gap through systematic phonics teaching.

Why ExcelKidsHub Was Started

  • Structured and systematic phonics instruction
  • Small batches for personalised attention
  • Step-by-step reading development
  • Measurable fluency improvement
  • Confidence-building learning environment

Our Teaching Approach

We follow a scientifically structured phonics progression that includes:

  • Letter sound mastery
  • Blending and segmenting techniques
  • Sight word recognition
  • Reading fluency development
  • Spelling accuracy
  • Reading comprehension skills
