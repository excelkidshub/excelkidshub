Our Specialized Programs

From Early Reading Foundation to Professional Teacher Certification

Level 1: Phonics Foundation (Age 4+)

This level introduces the 42 basic letter sounds of English using the multi-sensory Jolly Phonics method. We focus on decoding and letter recognition.

Core Curriculum:

  • Learning 42 letter sounds
  • Letter formation & penmanship
  • Blending sounds to read words
  • Segmenting sounds for spelling
  • Introduction to Tricky Words

Learning Outcome:

Children gain the confidence to read simple CVC words (like cat, hen, sun) and begin reading short, phonetic phrases independently.

Level 2: Advanced Phonics & Reading Fluency

Perfect for children who know basic sounds but struggle with complex spellings. We move into alternative vowel sounds and digraphs.

Advanced Topics:

  • Alternative vowel spellings (ai, ay, a-e)
  • Consonant blends and clusters
  • Reading multisyllabic words
  • Basic sentence structure & punctuation

Learning Outcome:

Students shift from "decoding" to "reading." They develop the speed and expression needed to enjoy reading storybooks without frustration.

Level 3: Grammar & Writing Mastery

Tailored for primary school students (Grade 1-5). We bridge the gap between spoken English and formal written English through the Jolly Grammar framework.

Grammar Pillars:

  • Nouns, Verbs, Adjectives & Adverbs
  • Punctuation & Capitalization
  • Tenses (Past, Present, Future)
  • Dictionary & Thesaurus usage
  • Creative story writing & Comprehension

Learning Outcome:

Students master school-level grammar rules, allowing them to excel in their English exams and express their thoughts clearly in writing.
