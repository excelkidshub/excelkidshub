Our Specialized Phonics Programs

From Early Reading Foundation to Professional Teacher Certification in Dhanori Pune

Level 1 – Phonics Foundation (Age 4+)

Introduction to 42 letter sounds using multi-sensory phonics methodology.

Core Curriculum

  • Learning 42 letter sounds
  • Letter formation & penmanship
  • Blending sounds to read words
  • Segmenting sounds for spelling
  • Introduction to Tricky Words

Learning Outcome

Children confidently read simple CVC words and short phonetic sentences independently.

Level 2 – Advanced Phonics & Reading Fluency

For children ready to move beyond basic decoding into fluent reading.

Advanced Topics

  • Alternative vowel spellings (ai, ay, a-e)
  • Consonant blends and clusters
  • Reading multisyllabic words
  • Sentence structure & punctuation

Learning Outcome

Students transition from decoding words to reading storybooks fluently with expression.

Level 3 – Grammar & Writing Mastery

Bridging spoken English and formal writing for primary school students.

Grammar Pillars

  • Nouns, Verbs, Adjectives & Adverbs
  • Punctuation & Capitalization
  • Tenses (Past, Present, Future)
  • Creative Writing & Comprehension

Learning Outcome

Students gain mastery in school-level grammar and confidently express ideas in writing.
