Phonics Curriculum

Step-by-step learning for every age group

🌱 Level 1: Basic

The foundation of reading. We cover the 42 letter sounds, proper letter formation, and the secret to blending sounds into words.

Focus: Sounds, Blending, Segmenting.

🚀 Level 2: Advanced

Taking reading to the next level by introducing alternative spellings for sounds and mastering "Tricky Words" that don't follow standard rules.

Focus: Complex spellings & Sentence formation.

📚 Level 3: Mastery

Our "Story Stars" program focuses on reading fluency, speed, and deep comprehension. Children start reading short stories independently.

Focus: Fluency, Comprehension, Vocabulary.
