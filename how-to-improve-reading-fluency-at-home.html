How to Improve Reading Fluency at Home

Practical daily habits to strengthen reading speed and understanding.

Reading fluency means reading accurately, smoothly and with expression. Parents can play a powerful role in building this skill at home.

1. Maintain a Daily Reading Routine

Just 10–15 minutes of focused reading every day builds steady improvement.

2. Encourage Repeated Reading

Reading the same short passage multiple times improves speed and accuracy.

3. Read Aloud Together

Listening to expressive reading helps children understand tone and rhythm.

4. Practise Sound Blends

Review common phonics patterns to strengthen decoding and fluency.

5. Celebrate Progress

Positive reinforcement builds confidence and motivation.
